We Give You the Options, You Vote On the Movies!

Choosing a movie for movie night can be hard, but we got you covered!

Step 1: Click "Select Number of Users" and click on the number of people that will be voting.

Step 2: Click "Generate Movies" so we can choose 4 movies for you and your friends or family to vote on.

Step 3: Each voter will click on their perspective button to vote on the movie that they want to watch and click "Done"

Step 4: Once everyone is done voting, click "Generate Result" to go to the next page and see which movie won!
